    About

    February 10, 2023
    Why studying Death and Dying? Philosophers and theologians around the globe have recognized a certain value to death in life. Death and life are bound together. Death brings new ideas and new ways through generational changes of values and ideals. Death also reminds us of our vulnerability, finitude and our resemblance as humans sharing the same fate. However, in our contemporary western society, the outlook on death has changed, with increased control over the end-of-life journey.
